Invoice App — Project Summary

Purpose:
A production-ready web invoicing platform with Greek myDATA mock integration, designed for freelancers and small businesses. It automates invoicing, tax validation, and document management while maintaining a modern, dark-mode responsive UI.

Key Highlights

Full Greek UI with myDATA simulation

Automated VAT and tax logic

PDF preview and export

Local data storage (browser-based)

Customer and company master data management

Real-time calculations and discount handling

Modal workflow and mobile-friendly layout

Technical Stack

Frontend: React 19.1.1 + TypeScript 5.8.3

Build Tool: Vite 7.1.2

Styling: TailwindCSS 3.4.17

Deployment: Vercel (Global CDN)

Performance: 1,206 LOC, 219 KB bundle, ~2.5s build time

Business Model & Market

Target: Greek SMEs and freelancers

Price: €399–€1,999 one-time or €29–€149/month subscription

Estimated Year 1 revenue: €130K–€300K

Long-term goal: Expansion to EU invoicing compliance and SaaS offering
